Kanban Coaching Professional Masterclass - San Diego, CA, USA - January 27-31 2014
The Kanban Coaching Professional leadership & coaching masterclass is for Kanban practitioners, consultants, coaches, change agents and managers. This masterclass is ideal for anyone tasked with leading a change initiative in their organization or at a client organization in the next year. You can spend a 3-day session or take both classes over 5 days with David J. Anderson.

Leading Change (Mon, Tues, Wed)

5-day class option
Kanban Coaching Professional Masterclass Comprehensive Course (Mon - Fri)
