We're pleased to announce that Digité is supporting the Regional Scrum Gathering®, India (RSGI) 2014 to be held next month - July 11, 12, in Hyderabad. With the theme of "Humane Scrum," RSGI '14 focuses on exploring techniques, tools, behaviors, and experiences that help bring about the mind-set so essential to reaping the benefits of using Scrum in organizations small and large.
