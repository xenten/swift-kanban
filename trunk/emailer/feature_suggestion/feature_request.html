Swift-Kanban Roadmap: Your chance to have your say!!
 
Thank you so much for signing up and trying out Swift-Kanban! We sincerely appreciate your giving us a chance and any feedback you may have shared with us. At Digite, we pride ourselves on engaging directly with our customers and users to shape our product roadmap. Based on your input in the past, we have added several prominent features including Smart-Lanes, Control-Charts, Notifications, Data Import/ Export features, and many more.

Even as we are working on already planned upgrades in the next few months, we would like to hear from you for our medium- and long-term roadmap planning. We invite you to participate in this feedback to us by voting on Swift-Kanban features that have already been requested or adding to the list with your own ideas! Based on the feedback we receive, and the popularity (number of votes) of each suggestion, we will strive to take as many of these suggestions into the roadmap for the next 6-9 months.
