Track, Measure and Improve productivity
 
We hope you've set up a Kanban board or two by now and have gotten a good understanding of most of its capabilities. One of the key areas where a Kanban system helps you is to understand your team's current capability - so that you can plan to make improvements from there. As they say, you can't improve what you can't measure!

The tutorial below is meant to help you learn to measure and improve productivity using SwiftKanban. The dashboard is designed to enable you to quickly configure and display metrics that you would usually monitor across projects. It gives you a glimpse of card metrics based on Priority, Release, Owner, and Estimates. Detailed information is available here.

To look at project-level metrics, simply go to your project and select Analytics from the drop-down menu next to your project name. Click the appropriate tab to see the appropriate metrics.

Some of the metrics available for your project are:
  1. Cumulative Flow Diagram
  2. Lead Time
  3. Cycle Time
  4. Work Time
  5. Wait Time
  6. Blocked Time
Details of each metrics can be found at our metric online help page.
