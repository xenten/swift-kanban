Swift-Kanban: Track, Measure and Improve productivity
 
Hi {{{first_name}}},

By now, you may have set up a Kanban board or two - and should have got a pretty good understanding of most of its capabilities. One of the key areas where a Kanban system helps you is to understand your team's current capability - so that you can plan to make improvements from there - as the say, you can't improve what you can't measure!!
  1. This tutorial below is meant to help you learn to measure and improve productivity using Swift-Kanban. The Swift-Kanban dashboard is simply designed to enable you to quickly configure and display metrics that you would usually monitor across projects. It gives you a quick glimpse of card metrics based on Prioritiy, Release, Owner, and Estimates. Detailed information here.
  2. To look at project-level metrics, simply go to your project and select Metrics from the Project Menu. Click the appropriate tab to view the corresponding metrics.
Some of the metrics available for your project are:
  1. Cumulative Flow Diagram
  2. Lead Time
  3. Cycle Time
  4. Work Time
  5. Wait Time
  6. Blocked Time
Details of each metrics can be found at our metric online help page.
