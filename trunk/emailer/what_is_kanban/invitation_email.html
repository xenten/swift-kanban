Have you heard about Kanban?

Kanban is a process that is gaining rapid adoption in IT organizations as an agile approach to deliver business value.

Organizations using Kanban say that it is a very effective approach - reasons often quoted for effectiveness are that it is simple to adopt, makes progress tracking visual and it gives them the ability to easily re-prioritize work depending on changing business needs.

Kanban is a common sense approach to getting work done:
  • Map the steps that any work goes through,
  • Visualize all your work against these steps,
  • Limit the amount of work that is concurrently in progress and
  • Eliminate any waste
Simple enough right? This is the basic foundation of Kanban.

Kanban empowers teams to become self organizing and this unleashes their true potential. A manager is able to take the role of a facilitator, leading the team to higher levels of performance.
