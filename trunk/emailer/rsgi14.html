Regional Scrum Gathering® India 2014
July 11, 12, Hyderabad





Hello,

We're pleased to announce that Digité is supporting the Regional Scrum Gathering®, India (RSGI) 2014 to be held next month - July 11, 12, in Hyderabad. With the theme of "Humane Scrum," RSGI '14 focuses on exploring techniques, tools, behaviors, and experiences that help bring about the mind-set so essential to reaping the benefits of using Scrum in organizations small and large.
 
 
 
Events at the Gathering
This will be a conclave of Scrum and Agile enthusiasts, CSTs and CSCs from across the world and particularly from South and South-east Asia. The conference will feature 5 session tracks, 35 presentations, 4 workshops and 3 keynotes. In addition there will be pre-conference workshops, a coaching clinic, panel discussion and more.
 
 
 
Why you should attend
To show our commitment to the Lean/ Agile community in India, we're offering you a 20% discount if you register on or before June 10, 2014 by using the discount code 'DIGITE'.

Also, Carol McEwan, MD of Scrum Alliance will be joining us at the conference. Delegates will also be eligible to claim 16 SEUs towards their CSP certification and 16 PDUs towards PMP Certification.
 
 
 
Kanbanize your team and boost its productivity!
This workshop will introduce participants to Kanban, its practices and its benefits. It will focus on how Kanban can be applied in your environments in a gradual, evolutionary manner. Participants will experience a simulated Kanban environment and understand how decision making changes when you think the Kanban way.
