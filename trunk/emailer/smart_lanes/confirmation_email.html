Webinar Details
If you have recently adopted Kanban to manage your work, one of the key challenges that you would have faced is mapping your value-stream with the lanes/ sub-lanes on a Kanban board. While your creativity might allow you to build great physical boards, working with physical board has its own limitation. Tools are necessary to scale up, especially if you have a distributed team. Tools help you to improve your productivity and efficiency.

But not many tools will let you build virtual boards with equal ease and provide the same amount of flexibility. This is where Swift-Kanban provides immense value. Swift-Kanban allows you translate your creativity in to a Virtual Kanban Board using Smart-Lanes i.e. lanes with intelligence built in. Smart-Lanes allow you to define Swim-Lanes based on an array of attributes, which then self-govern the position of card, based on the attribute value of the card.

In this webinar, we'll demonstrate how you can use Smart-Lanes available in Swift-Kanban to model your Kanban Board for your specific value-stream and various other features available in Swift-Kanban to define your Kanban Board to manage your work.

In this one hour webinar, join our experts as they share their experience with such initiatives to decide for yourself what would work within your organization!
