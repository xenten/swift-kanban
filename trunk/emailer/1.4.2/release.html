Swift-Kanban Feature Updates: Feb 27th, 2012
Right on the heels of Swift-Kanban v1.4 that was released at the start of the year, we have pushed a few nifty features in last few weeks to help you use the application better. Some of the features introduced are:-

Better Offline Collaboration

You can now keep track of collaboration comments added against any card even in offline mode, that are now included as a part of the card export csv/ excel file. As you discuss the cards offline with your team, you may even add a new comment in the file and import it back to Swift-Kanban to add new comments against cards.

Easier Status Tracking

You can now view the latest comment against any card by simply hovering on the ‘Comments’ icon. This could be very handy for your daily stand-ups and will save multiple ‘Clicks’ to check the latest comment on any card.

Quick Addition of New Users

The user addition view has been simplified further and allows creation of new users by providing minimum required details. Rest of the profile information can be updated by the users themselves on login.

Improved Analytics for Imported Cards

The ‘Lead Time’ metric is now computed based on a new field ‘Date Identified’, that has been introduced to retain the creation dates for any card that are being imported to Swift-Kanban from any external system. This will help you to bring-in your “In-Progress” data anytime and still get an accurate lead time for the imported cards.

We heartily thank all our users who directly or indirectly, provided their valuable feedback to bring out the above features. We look forward to your continued patronage to help us improve the product further.
