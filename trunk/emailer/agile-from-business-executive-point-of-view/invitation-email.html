An Introduction to Agile from a
Business/ Executive Point of View
This webinar focuses on the benefits you get from Agile, not the how of doing it. Agile is really about delivering business value incrementally, quickly and predictably. It should not be about development iterations. Starting with the "how" of Agile instead of the "why" of Agile often leaves many business stakeholders unclear with the need for Agile.
