Is Kanban Applicable for my work?

Why should I care about Kanban? You've heard about Kanban. With all the buzzwords floating around, it is easy to ignore Kanban! “Here we go again!!” Thankfully, all buzzwords are not equal and Kanban is worth your time.

Kanban is a practical approach not only to get work done, but to be able to prioritize work that delivers maximum value and ensure everyone has the same expectation of “what, why and when”. And Kanban empowers teams to be self-directing and responsible for their deliverables. End of finger pointing... period!

Kanban's emphasis on a visual paradigm to see work that is being done makes it really effective. If it stares you in your face, it gets attention! Work, bottlenecks, waste and your commitments demand your attention and serve as leading indicators of potential issues downstream, enabling your team to take action in real time. Much better than a postmortem after things go horribly wrong, right?!
