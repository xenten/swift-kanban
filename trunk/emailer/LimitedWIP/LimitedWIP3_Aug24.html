Limited WIP Society India (Bangalore Chapter) - 3rd Meetup

The Limited WIP Society India (Bangalore Chapter) is planning its 3rd Meetup this Saturday, the 24th of August, 2013. We will focus on Agile Requirements in this meetup. User story mapping is one of the popular techniques to define requirements that follow the INVEST model. Accordingly, the agenda for this meetup will be as follows:

2nd session: User Story Mapping - 90min - Manik.
