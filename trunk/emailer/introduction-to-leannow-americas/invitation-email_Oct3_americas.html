Introduction to LeanNow™
The first webinar in this series stresses the importance of focusing on flow instead of waste reduction as a means to achieving a rapid Lean implementation. After attending this webinar, you will be able evaluate the appropriateness of Lean for your organization, compare LeanNow™ to other approaches, and share the underlying principles with others.
