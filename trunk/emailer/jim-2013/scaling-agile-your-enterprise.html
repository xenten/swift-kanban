Scaling Agile in your Enterprise – the Journey is Personal!

Kanban is an evolutionary (non-disruptive) change management process; the fact is that it requires a change in organizational and management practices and behavior that can be daunting. The best place to begin that change is at a personal level. One of the main goals of having a Personal Kanban is the adoption of a "kaizen attitude". This is an attitude of continuous improvement.
