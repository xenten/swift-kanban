Kanban in procurement with Clarinha Prado

In this webinar Clarinha will go through the successful use of SwiftKanban for improvement of the procurement process for Poiesis - a non-governmental organization in São Paulo, Brazil which promotes socio-cultural and educational development, focused on project management in cultural spaces.

Poiesis’ procurement process faced challenges such as lack of transparency, delays in the procurement process, lack of understanding of business rules, among others. Join us to find out how Kanban helped.
