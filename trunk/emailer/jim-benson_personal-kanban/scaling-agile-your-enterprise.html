A Critical Tool for Enterprise Agility – Personal Kanban!

At Digite, we are always looking for ways to help our valued customers achieve greater productivity, efficiency and customer value. Last year, we launched Swift-Kanban, a visual, Lean/ Kanban based project management tool.

Kanban is an evolutionary (non-disruptive) change management process and meant to be easy to implement. However, the fact is that it requires a change in organizational and management practices and behavior that can be daunting. The best place to begin that change is at a personal level. One of the main goals of having personal Kanban is to adapt to an attitude of continuous improvement.

We are deeply honored to have Jim Benson – widely recognized as the father of Personal Kanban – write a guest blog – Implementing Kanban? Start with Yourself! – for us that emphasizes precisely that and helps you and your colleagues get started on a continuous improvement journey!
