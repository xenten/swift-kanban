Scaling Agile in your Enterprise – the Journey is Personal!

At Digité, we are proud of the products we bring to you and the satisfaction that you, the customer, have derived from them. Swift-Kanban – the Lean/ Agile Project Management tool that we introduced last year has helped a wide variety of organizations adopt Lean and Agile principles in their software and IT organizations. The interest in Kanban is growing steadily and rapidly as a critical tool to enable Lean and Agile at the Enterprise level!

While Kanban is an evolutionary (non-disruptive) change management process, the fact is that it requires a change in organizational and management practices and behavior that can be daunting. The best place to begin that change is at a personal level.

We are deeply honored to have Jim Benson – the father of Personal Kanban - write a guest blog for us that emphasizes precisely that and helps you get started!

 
Since Tonianne and I started writing about Personal Kanban four years ago, we've been gratified and amazed with both the rapid adoption and the expressiveness with which people create their boards. Beyond that, it's been wonderful to watch tools like Swift Kanban take the ideas and create convenient tools that busy people can use to visualize their work and limit their work-in-progress (WIP).

The ideas behind Personal Kanban are simple, we want to:
  • visualize our work because we can better manage what we can see, and
  • limit our work-in-progress because we cannot do more work than we can handle.
