Hello,

We're excited to conduct a webinar that's based on the successful use of SwiftKanban for improvement of the procurement process for the Brazilian organization Poiesis. Our presenter for the session will be Clarinha Prado, a consultant with Gigante Consultoria, Brazil. Clarinha has over 20 years of Business Analysis and IT consulting experience in a variety of fields, especially the financial sector.

Poiesis is a non-governmental organization in São Paulo, Brazil which promotes socio-cultural and educational development, focused on project management in cultural spaces. Their procurement process faced challenges such as lack of transparency, delays in the procurement process, lack of understanding of business rules, among others. After an initial analysis of the challenges faced, Gigante decided on the use of Kanban to help them improve the overall procurement process. In Phase 1, they implemented a physical Kanban system; in Phase 2, after an exhaustive comparison of several electronic Kanban tools, they selected SwiftKanban to automate the Kanban system.
