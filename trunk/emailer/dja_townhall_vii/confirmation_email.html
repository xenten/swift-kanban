Webinar Details: State of Kanban 2012 - Town-hall with David Anderson!
Date: Nov 08, 2012; 09:00 AM Pacific

We are back once again with a "State of the Union" styled update from David Anderson himself! He will provide highlights of Lean/ Kanban worldwide, especially in Europe following the 3 LKU Kanban conferences. He will also provide a summary of Kanban related training and certification options with Lean Kanban University.

This will be followed by a Q&A session where you get to ask him questions directly. In this popular series, David has answered questions such as:
  1. I am working with Scrum/ Iterative Development. Is Kanban for me?
  2. We have setup our Kanban board - so, now what?
  3. What are some common ways to handle rework and defects?
  4. Can I do without estimates in Kanban?
  5. How can I use Kanban metrics more effectively?
If you relate to these questions and have others like these, come join us and ask!
