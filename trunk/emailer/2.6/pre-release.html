SwiftKanban Feature Update
We have exciting news for all our users! As many of you may already know, we are on the verge of announcing the release of our much-awaited SwiftKanban release version 2.6, this weekend!

We are sending you this mailer to provide you a heads-up into what you can expect to see, especially to help you navigate the new UI. A detailed email will follow over the weekend to give you a more comprehensive list of enhancements!

Here is a quick peek into its key unrivalled features:

Revamped Contemporary User Interface
We have listened to feedback from our users and have completely revamped the UI for a more modern look and feel, a more pleasing combination of colors and more color theme choices to the user – with many more to follow!




More Space for your Board!!
One of the biggest challenges teams have when switching from a physical board to a virtual board is the ability to view as much of the board as possible within the confines of a screen. Again, we have heard your feedback and made a number of changes to expand the viewable area of the board as much as possible!




New Features!
In addition to UI improvements, we are introducing the most collaborative, most real-time Kanban board yet – to enable you to stay in-sync with your colleagues and customers at all times!

Here’s a quick list -
• Real-time collaboration with board-level and card-level chat!
• Stay in sync with your team members at all times with Auto-Board Refresh and Instant Notifications!
• Manage your backlog better on the Backlog Board! Easily pull in cards from the configurable Backlog to the Kanban Board

Stay tuned for the Release announcement and get your hands on the new version.

Do continue to send us your feedback and suggestions at support@swift-kanban.com. You may even post them on the Swift-Kanban Community page.
