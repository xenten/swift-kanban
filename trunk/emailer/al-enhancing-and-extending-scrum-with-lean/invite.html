Enhancing and Extending Scrum With Lean
Enhancing and Extending Scrum With Lean is the sixth webinar in our "Business and Agile" webinar series. This webinar discusses why Scrum must be enhanced and extended with Lean to work well on all but small projects. Incorporating Lean into Scrum can both improve the teams' abilities as well as enable teams to work together better.
