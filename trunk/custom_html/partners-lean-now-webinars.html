Introduction to LeanNow™
This is the best way to find out about LeanNow and how it can work for your organization. This 50-minute webinar is designed for anyone interested in rapidly applying Lean in their organization. It is appropriate for owners and top executives, department and division heads, Lean practitioners, implementation teams, and Lean team members. After attending this webinar, you will be able evaluate the appropriateness of Lean for your organization, compare LeanNow to other approaches, and share the underlying principles with others.

Getting Started With LeanNow™
If you're considering LeanNow for your organization, you'll want to know how LeanNow is deployed. You will want to understand what resources are needed and what the implementation timeline would look like. You may also want to know how LeanNow implementation differs fro traditional Lean. After attending this webinar, you will understand what it takes to implement LeanNow, be prepared to describe the implementation process and be able to start planning your organization's implementation. If your organization is already implementing LeanNow, you will be better grounded in the implementation process.

Managing with LeanNow™
In this webinar we show you how to drive Lean results to the bottom line within weeks. We'll show you how to avoid the biggest mistake of Lean organizations: working on waste before flow. Here you'll learn how to use new technologies to build flow first, drive results using Lean management and use flow to identify and eliminate waste. After attending this webinar, you will have a basic understanding of the tactics and activities needed to manage daily flow with LeanNow principles. You'll understand the importance of working on flow first and how to use flow to continuously improve Lean outcomes.

Building Great KanBan Boards for LeanNow™
For 60 years, Lean was a physical approach. It required time and resources to first reorganize the workplace, remove waste, and set up physical flow. LeanNow has changed that. Now we can implement flow first and then use it to manage lean results, remove waste with precision, and tighten flow. The electronic Kanban board is a critical technology. This webinar explains how to set up a Kanban board. After attending this webinar, you will have the basic information needed to set up a successful electronic Kanban board for use with LeanNow.
