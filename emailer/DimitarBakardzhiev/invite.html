Hello,

Most project teams face two major hurdles when planning - estimating the budget and the delivery time. Management has to plan in order to make sure they have the financial means and the resources for all the projects the company has.

Teams have to work with a combination of a high-level and a low-level plan. While the low-level plans get done closer to the actual project events, the high-level plan must recognize the variability and randomness inherent in every project. So, teams can't control the Waves of Uncertainty, but they must learn how to Surf!

In this webinar, which is the second in a webinar series in collaboration with LeanKanban University (LKU), Dimitar Bakardzhiev will introduce you to a probabilistic approach for preparing high-level plans. Based on existing historical statistical data recorded and utilizing Monte Carlo simulation, predictions can be made that could be used for planning new projects.

Dimitar is the Managing Director of Taller Technologies Bulgaria and an expert in driving successful and cost-effective technology development. He is a Lean-Kanban University Accredited Kanban Trainer (AKT) and avid, expert Kanban practitioner.
